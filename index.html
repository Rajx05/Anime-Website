Jujutsu Kaisen Characters

Yuji Itadori

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a first-year student at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College. He is a kind-hearted and selfless individual who values human life above all else. Yuji is also a natural-born athlete with exceptional physical abilities. He is the host of Sukuna, the King of Curses, and possesses immense cursed energy.
